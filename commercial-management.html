COMMERCIAL MANAGEMENT

Commercial Management

In addition to the commercial management of its own fleet, Navitramp and its affiliates have been chartering in third party tonnage for over 20 years.

Over this period a reputation has been built for reliability and performance with both owners whose vessels are chartered in and clients whose cargoes are carried on these chartered in vessels.

Vessels are chartered in from a wide range of owners including public listed companies and major Greek owners.

Services are provided to a large number of major international cargo owners with whom close relationships have been established over a number of years.

Vessels are employed either on time charter, short term or period, or voyage basis, single or multiple depending upon owners’ needs and market conditions.

Third Party Commercial Management

We are also able to provide third party management for dry cargo vessels ranging in size from coastal to Ultramax to those owners who prefer to outsource this aspect of management.

Our experienced team of brokers is fully supported by dedicated operators who have a large network of port agents, suppliers, surveyors, bunker brokers/suppliers as well as access to weather monitoring in order to ensure the most economical operation of the vessel. Our expertise flows through to our accounting system and staff that enables us to keep owners fully up to date with all financial aspects.

We are able to maximise an owners return by the combination of our chartering teams expertise in sourcing best available charters thus ensuring that freight indexes are constantly exceeded, coupled with strong control of all operational expenses.
