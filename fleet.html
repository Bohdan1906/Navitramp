FLEET

The fleet managed both technically and commercially by Navitramp and its affiliates consists of 7 dry cargo vessels ranging in size from about 10,887 dwt to 58,721 dwt.

Navitramp has established high standards with regard to the maintenance of the fleet keeping the vessels in an excellent condition.

The technical management is carried out by highly qualified individuals, including Engineer and Marine superintendents, who identify any possible need for improvement, at an early stage, through frequent inspections.
